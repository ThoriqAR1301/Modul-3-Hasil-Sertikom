Eiffel Tower

Paris, France

The Eiffel Tower is a famous wrought iron structure located in Paris, France. Built in 1889 for the World's Fair, the tower stands 330 meters tall and is an iconic symbol of French culture and innovation. Visitors can enjoy stunning views of the city from its observation deck

Pura Ulun Danau Beratan

Bali, Indonesia

Ulun Danu Beratan Temple is a beautiful temple located on the shores of Lake Beratan, Bedugul, Bali. Built in the 17th century, this temple is dedicated to Dewi Danu, the goddess of water, lakes, and rivers. Its beauty lies in the serene view of the lake with a mountainous backdrop, making it one of Bali's tourist icons

Shibuya Crossing

Tokyo, Japan

Shibuya Crossing is an iconic intersection in Tokyo, Japan, known as one of the busiest in the world. Surrounded by large advertising screens and modern buildings, it is a symbol of Tokyo's dynamic and always bustling city life

Times Square

New York, USA

Times Square is an iconic entertainment and business center in New York City, known for its bright neon lights, large digital screens, Broadway theaters, and always-bustling atmosphere. It is a symbol of dynamic city life and a major tourist attraction

Grand Canal

Venice, Italy

The Grand Canal is the main canal in Venice, Italy, which runs through the city and is approximately 3.8 km long. Surrounded by historic buildings, the canal is an iconic transportation route using gondolas and vaporettos, offering a romantic view of Venice

Manchester Town Hall

Manchester, England

Manchester Town Hall is a historic building located in the city centre of Manchester, England. Built in the 19th century, the Neo-Gothic building is known for its magnificent architecture and iconic clock tower. Manchester Town Hall is an important symbol of the city's history and pride, and is often used for official events and government meetings
